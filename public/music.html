FACILITY 64 — COMPOSE THE IN-GAME SOUNDTRACK

Click cells to place notes/hits. LEAD and BASS rows are a pitch grid — click a row+step to set that pitch.
LEAD (8 pitch rows) and BASS (5 pitch rows) are monophonic — one note per step, click again to clear, click a different row to change pitch.
KICK / SNARE / HAT are on/off hits. SAVE TO SERVER makes the track selectable from the MUSIC dropdown in the game menu. OPEN GAME →
